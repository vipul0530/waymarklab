Product

Continuum — SAR-7 Outreach Automation

Continuum helps county social services agencies keep eligible families enrolled in CalFresh by automating SAR-7 outreach before deadlines are missed — reducing churn, lowering caseworker burden, and keeping benefits flowing to the families who need them most.

  • Automated outreach via SMS, email, and mail before SAR-7 deadlines
  • Direct integration with CalSAWS and BenefitsCal platforms
  • Caseworker dashboard with real-time household status tracking
  • Multilingual communication templates for diverse populations
