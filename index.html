Why Waymark Lab

Built Different. Built for Social Services.

We don't adapt commercial software for county welfare departments — we build from the ground up with California HHS operations, compliance requirements, and caseworker workflows at the center.

01

Benefits Administration Experts

We understand the complexity of California's benefits ecosystem — CalSAWS, LEADER, C-IV, SAR-7 deadlines, and mid-year status reports. Built by people who have managed these workflows from inside county government.

HIPAA & PHI Security

Benefits data contains some of the most sensitive PII in the world. Our architecture is HIPAA-aware by design, with PHI protection, audit trails, and data isolation built in from day one — not bolted on.

CalSAWS & BenefitsCal Native

Our integrations are purpose-built for California's eligibility infrastructure. We know LEADER, C-IV, CalSAWS, and BenefitsCal — and we build to work with them, not around them.
