About Us

Government Technology Built from the Inside Out

We didn't come to government technology from the outside. We built careers inside government agencies — and we started Waymark Lab because we knew the technology could be better.

Our Team

Public Sector Veterans. Technology Builders.

Waymark Lab was founded by public sector technology veterans with decades of combined experience building and managing enterprise software systems for California government agencies.

We understand the constraints, the compliance requirements, and the real-world challenges that government technology teams face — because we have lived them. We've sat in the county offices, navigated the procurement processes, and shipped software that had to work in environments far more demanding than any commercial deployment.

That experience is baked into everything we build. It's why our products integrate with CalSAWS on day one. It's why our security posture is designed around HIPAA and CJIS from the ground up. And it's why county IT teams trust us to handle the data of the citizens they serve.

Our Mission

Mission Statement

Our Values

What We Stand For

Four principles guide every product decision, every customer conversation, and every line of code we ship.

Integrity

We tell county partners what will work and what won't. We don't oversell, we don't overpromise, and we don't ship software we don't believe in.

Security

The data we handle belongs to real people. We treat it that way — with enterprise-grade security, rigorous access controls, and zero tolerance for compromise.

Impact

We measure success by outcomes for citizens — families kept enrolled in benefits, caseworkers freed from unnecessary administrative work, agencies running more efficiently.

Partnership

We work alongside county agencies as long-term partners, not one-time vendors. Your operational success is our success — we're invested in it for the long haul.
