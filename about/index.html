Our Values

What We Stand For

These aren't aspirational wall words. They're the principles that drive every product decision we make.

Integrity First

We don't oversell, over-promise, or under-deliver. Government agencies need partners they can trust — and trust is built through honesty about what we can and cannot do.

Security Without Compromise

Sensitive citizen data is not negotiable. We build every system as if we are personally responsible for every record it contains — because we believe we are.

Real Impact

Our measure of success is whether a CalFresh family kept their benefits, or a caseworker finished their day without burning out. Not lines of code or features shipped.

Deep Partnership

We don't sell software and disappear. We embed with county teams, learn the culture, understand the constraints, and stay through the hard parts of implementation.

Our Approach

Government Experience Is Not Optional

There's a long history of technology companies entering the government market with commercial tools and impressive pitch decks — only to underestimate the complexity of public sector operations. Procurement constraints. Legacy system dependencies. Regulatory compliance. Union considerations. Budget cycles. We know all of it.

Our founders have worked inside county agencies, inside state-level programs, and alongside the caseworkers who manage California's most critical social safety net programs. We build software informed by that experience — not by assumptions.

That means slower, more deliberate product development. More stakeholder engagement. More time understanding the problem before writing a line of code. And better outcomes because of it.
