About Waymark Lab

Built From the Inside Out

Waymark Lab was founded by public sector technology veterans with decades of experience building and managing enterprise software systems inside California county social services agencies. We have worked alongside eligibility workers, supervised technology teams serving hundreds of thousands of CalFresh and CalWORKs cases, and navigated the real-world complexity of CalSAWS, CDSS compliance, and county procurement — because we have lived it from the inside.

"To build technology that helps California counties serve more families, reduce administrative burden, and ensure that eligible residents receive the benefits they've earned — without unnecessary paperwork getting in the way."

— Waymark Lab Mission

Our Values

What We Stand For

These aren't aspirational wall words. They're the principles that drive every product decision we make.

Integrity First

We don't oversell, over-promise, or under-deliver. Government agencies need partners they can trust — and trust is built through honesty about what we can and cannot do.

Security Without Compromise

Sensitive citizen data is not negotiable. We build every system as if we are personally responsible for every record it contains — because we believe we are.

Real Impact

Our measure of success is whether a CalFresh family kept their benefits, or a caseworker finished their day without burning out. Not lines of code or features shipped.

Deep Partnership

We don't sell software and disappear. We embed with county teams, learn the culture, understand the constraints, and stay through the hard parts of implementation.

Our Approach

HHS Experience Is Not Optional

There's a long history of technology companies entering the benefits administration market with commercial tools and impressive pitch decks — only to underestimate the complexity of county social services operations. CalSAWS dependencies. SAR-7 deadline cycles. HIPAA requirements. State compliance mandates. Union considerations. Budget constraints. We know all of it — because we've lived it.

Our founders have worked inside county DPSS agencies, inside state-level benefits programs, and alongside the caseworkers who manage California's most critical social safety net. We build software informed by that experience — not by assumptions.

That means slower, more deliberate product development. More time inside your eligibility workflows before writing a line of code. More stakeholder engagement. And better outcomes for caseworkers and the families they serve.
