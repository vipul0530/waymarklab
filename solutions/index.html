Benefits Administration Platform

Continuum

Managing California's CalFresh SAR-7 Interim Report is one of the most administratively intensive tasks in county government. Continuum makes it manageable for caseworkers and accessible for families.

  • Mobile-first, plain-language SAR-7 form for any device
  • Multi-language support with accessibility compliance
  • Intelligent deadline tracking with automated reminders
  • Document upload with automated validation and routing
  • Caseworker queue, dashboard, and case management tools
  • Native integration with LEADER, C-IV, and CalSAWS
  • HIPAA-aware — hosted on Azure Government Cloud

Our Process

How We Build Government Software

Every solution starts with people — the caseworkers, residents, and administrators who will live inside it every day.

Discover

We embed with your team — observing real workflows, interviewing caseworkers, and mapping the actual pain points in your operations.

Design

Co-design with your stakeholders. We build prototypes, test with real users, and validate solutions before writing a single line of production code.

Integrate

We connect with your existing systems — LEADER, C-IV, CalSAWS, and legacy platforms — so your team never has to choose between old and new.

Deploy

Phased rollout with training, change management, and ongoing support. We stay through go-live and beyond to make sure it sticks.
