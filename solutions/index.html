Continuum

SAR-7 Outreach Automation

Continuum helps county social services agencies keep eligible families enrolled in CalFresh by automating SAR-7 outreach before deadlines are missed. Fewer terminations, less re-enrollment paperwork, and better outcomes for the families your agency serves.

  • Automated SMS, email, and mail outreach before SAR-7 deadlines
  • Native integration with CalSAWS and BenefitsCal
  • Real-time household status dashboard for caseworkers
  • Multilingual communication templates
  • Full audit log and compliance reporting
  • Configurable outreach cadence per county policy

Mosaic

Multi-Program Benefits Navigator

Mosaic is a multi-program benefits eligibility navigator built for caseworkers. It surfaces relevant program information, eligibility rules, and cross-program interactions across the full landscape of California public benefits — so caseworkers can serve clients faster and more accurately.

  • Unified view across CalFresh, Medi-Cal, CalWORKs, and more
  • Real-time eligibility rule updates as policy changes
  • Cross-program interaction alerts for caseworkers
  • Integrates with CalSAWS workflows

Sentinel

Fraud & Anomaly Detection

Sentinel brings intelligent anomaly detection to public benefits programs. It surfaces unusual patterns in case data — duplicate records, inconsistent household data, suspicious activity — so county fraud investigators can focus their time where it matters most.

  • Automated anomaly detection across case records
  • Risk scoring and investigation queue management
  • Duplicate record identification and flagging
  • Audit-ready investigation documentation
